Productbeschrijving

FIFA 23 features improved HyperMotion2 technology with double the motion capture for more realistic animations. Play in both men's and women's FIFA World Cup tournaments, with new women's club teams and cross-play functionality. Experience new customization options and gameplay improvements in VOLTA FOOTBALL and Profclubs. FIFA 23 includes over 19,000 players, 700 teams, 100+ stadiums, and 30+ leagues, such as the UEFA Champions League and Premier League. Customers in Belgium can also subscribe to Eleven - All Access for access to documentaries and other content, with monthly renewals and the ability to cancel at any time with one month's notice. Customers who purchase FIFA 23 in-store will receive an Eleven Month Pass code card, while those who purchase for home delivery will receive a pass by email while supplies last.
